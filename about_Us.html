Clement Motloutsi

I was born in Tzaneen, Limpopo and I went to Tshwane university of Technology to further my studies.
As I was busy with my studies, I was so fascinated by the relationship between human and computers.
I kept asking myself how computers follow exactly what they are being instructed to do.

I wanted to understand what kind of procedure do they go through to achieve the stated instructions.
As I researched, I found out that they have their own language that can be used by human to communicate with them.
They simply follow a set of instruction of a program.

I realised that computers makes life much easier and if used effectively, they can solve most of our daily problems.
I was so interested in building my own computer program that can solve real life problems but I didn’t know how to do it.
So I decided to learn how to code but I struggled as I didn’t have any guidance on how to do it and I mostly relied on You tube.
I tried but I had to quit as I felt like it was not for me.

My interest for building stuff did not end. I went back to learning but I still struggled.
I realised that I did not have enough time to learn so many things as I was busy with my studies on the other side.
I tried building few projects but I struggled. I didn’t know how to start and in what manner.
I will continue learning and this time around, I will learn by building projects.

Thank you for reading
